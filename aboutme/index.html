Hello Everyone, My name is Toakase Tu'ihalangingie, and my nickname is "Tia." I'm married to the love of my life. We were both raised and born on the Island of Tonga, but we currently live in Orem, Utah. My major is Applied Technology. I started taking this course last semester, and my mother passed, and I dropped all my classes. Despite the challenge I faced, I'm truly grateful for a loving Heavenly Father who is there for me during the most hardship in my life.

Furthermore, I'm thankful for the gospel of Jesus Christ and its teaching, especially for the plan of happiness. I found much comfort in it, knowing that I could see my mother again. I am grateful for the opportunity to be here to study and learn from you all.

I am looking forward to working with you all this semester. Best of luck to you all.
